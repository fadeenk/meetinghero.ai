What calendars do you support?

Account & Privacy

How do you protect my data?

We use industry-standard encryption and never share your data. You control which calendars and information are synced.

Can I exclude internal colleagues?

Yes, you can exclude specific domains (like your company email) from prep reports in your settings.

Can I choose what information I receive?

Absolutely! You can customize which types of information are included in your prep documents.

We strive for accuracy by using reputable sources, but always recommend verifying critical details yourself.
