Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22nd, 2025

We at Internet Candy Corporation (the "Company", "we", "us", or "our") know you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy seriously. We consider it very important to limit who can see your personal information and to be transparent about how we use it.

What information do we collect?

Information you provide to us

We receive and store any information you knowingly provide to us. For example, through the registration process and/or through your account settings, we may collect Personal Information such as your name, email address, phone number, etc. If you sign in to the Services through a third party site or service, you understand some content and/or information in those accounts ("Third Party Account Information") may be transmitted into your account with us if you authorize such transmissions.

Information Collected Automatically

Whenever you interact with our Services, we automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser or device, which may include your IP address, geolocation data, device identification, "cookie" information, and the type of browser and/or device you're using to access our Services.

How Is the Data Collected Being Used?

We use the data we collect to provide, improve, and customize our services for you. Specifically:

  • • Authentication: We use your name and email address to verify your identity and securely log you into our app.
  • • Calendar Scanning: We access your calendar information to identify events and provide the services you have requested.
  • • Calendar Updates: With your permission, we use write access to your calendar to deliver requested services.

Will you share any of the Personal Information?

We will never disclose the data we collect from you to third parties unless it is for the delivery of the service. We may only disclose Personal Information that we collect as described in this privacy policy:

  • • Subsidiaries and affiliates who are bound by our privacy standards
  • • Service providers who support our business and are bound by confidentiality
  • • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights

Is Personal Information about me secure?

For your privacy and security, your account is protected by authentication through your calendar provider. We do not store or have access to your calendar provider password. Unauthorized access to your account is directly tied to the security of your calendar provider account.

We endeavor to protect the privacy of your account and other Personal Information we hold in our records, but unfortunately, no one can absolutely guarantee complete security.
